  • Stress_TN

    In 2006 Stanislav and his best friend create project “8bit brothers” which play only Fackebit. 8bb have many free and payable albums on Itunes, Beatport etc. Hard styles and happy tunes makes “Brothers” very popular in Russia.

    In 2008 Stanislav start his own project, named “Stress_TN”. Huge arsenal of music gadgets and consoles provided new opportunities for the musician. For South of Russia it was a new wave of experimental music. Game Boys and Commodore turned into a weapon of mass destruction on the dance floor into his hand. He has many albums, EP's, covers and etc

    Since 2000 year Stress_TN became a sound produser and composer in Kintogames. All games with Stress_TN music you can find on Kintogames.com.

    Now Stress_TN is the administrator of the group “LSDJ in RUSSIA”. This group help’s young chipmusician.
